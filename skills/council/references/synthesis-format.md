# Synthesis Format

The synthesis document has exactly 6 sections:

## 1. Consensus
Points where all or most perspectives agreed. These represent low-controversy
decisions that can likely proceed without further debate.

- List only genuine agreement
- Be specific (not "everyone thinks security matters")
- Separate agreement on facts from agreement on recommendations

## 2. Key Tensions
Fundamental disagreements between perspectives. For each tension:
- What the disagreement is about
- Which perspectives are on each side
- The strongest argument from each side

Do not invent compromise if perspectives didn't converge. Explain **why**
they disagree (different risk tolerance, different information, different values).

## 3. Risk Assessment
Material risks of each decision path, mapped to the perspectives that
identified them.

- Group risks by decision path, not by severity
- Include both technical and organizational risks

## 4. Recommended Path Forward
A concrete recommendation with reasoning.

- Best risk/reward profile, not a hollow compromise
- Specific and actionable
- Explain why this balances the perspectives
- Acknowledge trade-offs being accepted

## 5. Minority Report
Important dissenting positions not adopted in the recommendation.

- Only substantive dissent
- Explain what risk or concern is unaddressed
- Include alternative actions if the dissenter offered them

## 6. Suggested Actions
Numbered, concrete next steps if the recommendation is accepted.

- Each action starts with a responsible party
- Include completion criteria or timeline
- Sequence matters: list in execution order

## Rules

- Do not invent arguments that weren't made
- Do not soften disagreements — if irreconcilable, say so
- Do not editorialise — report and structure, don't judge
- Attribute positions to specific perspectives
- Keep total synthesis under 1500 words

## Length Guidelines

- Consensus: 100-200 words
- Key Tensions: 300-400 words (largest section)
- Risk Assessment: 200-300 words
- Recommended Path Forward: 200-300 words
- Minority Report: 100-200 words
- Suggested Actions: 100-200 words
